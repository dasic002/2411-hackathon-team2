FAQ

Is the site compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies?

Yes, our website is fully compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies. We utilize semantic HTML and ARIA roles to ensure seamless navigation for visually impaired users.

How can I use voice commands to navigate the site?

Voice commands are supported through compatible browsers and devices. You can activate these features in your device settings for effortless, hands-free navigation.

Can I receive automatic updates for weather and news?

We currently don't offer real-time updates when you access the site. Automatic push notifications for updates will be part of future enhancements based on user feedback.

What should I do if I experience difficulties using the website?

If you encounter any issues or have feedback, please visit the "Contact" section for support options. Our team is dedicated to continuously improving accessibility based on user experiences.

Where can I find information that keeps me up to date about technology for the visually impaired?

Visit the Lighthouse Guild which provides a great portal of information including technology available and a pod cast series about technology.

Where can I find a step by step guide to assistive aids and technology?

Check out Royal National Institute of Blind People. The RNIB is the UK's leading sight loss charity. They offer practical and emotional support to blind and partially sighted people, their families and carers.

How can I change my font size on my screen?

Apple Devices: Press 'command' and the '+' symbol to make screen larger. Press 'command' and the '-' symbol to decrease screen size.

iPhones: To adjust the font size on your mobile phone. Go to 'Settings' - Tap 'Accessibility' - Tap 'Display & Text Size' - Tap 'Larger Text'. Tap the switch to turn on 'Larger Accessibility Sizes' to expand the range of text sizes. Use the slider to adjust text size

Apple Macs and desktops/laptops:

Go to 'System Settings' - Click 'Accessibility' in the sidebar - click 'Display' situated on the right. You may need to scroll down. Change the settings you require and exit.

Android Devices: Select 'Settings' - then 'Display' - select 'Font size' adjust as required. - select 'Display size' use slider to adjust, this will change the size of your app icons.

Android Devices: Select 'Settings' - then 'Display' - select 'Font size' adjust as required. - select 'Display size' use slider to adjust, this will change the size of your app icons

Windows: Type 'Display' into your start bar. Select 'Change the resolution of the display' A display settings screen will now display to allow you to change font size, icon size as required.

Can I change the default voice used?

Yes you can change the voice used, simply select the options from the drop down arrow in the voice section.

How is my personal information handled, especially when using location services?

Your privacy is our priority. Location data, if used, is handled with strict confidentiality and solely for providing accurate weather information. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
