FAQ

Is the site compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies?

Yes, our website is fully compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies. We utilize semantic HTML and ARIA roles to ensure seamless navigation for visually impaired users.

How can I use voice commands to navigate the site?

Voice commands are supported through compatible browsers and devices. You can activate these features in your device settings for effortless, hands-free navigation.

Can I receive automatic updates for weather and news?

We currently don't offer real-time updates when you access the site. Automatic push notifications for updates will be part of future enhancements based on user feedback.

How do I adjust the text size and contrast settings?

Our website offers customizable settings to adjust text size and contrast. Simply navigate to the "Settings" section in the header or footer to personalize your viewing experience according to your needs.

How is my personal information handled, especially when using location services?

Your privacy is our priority. Location data, if used, is handled with strict confidentiality and solely for providing accurate weather information. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.

What should I do if I experience difficulties using the website?

If you encounter any issues or have feedback, please visit the "Contact" section for support options. Our team is dedicated to continuously improving accessibility based on user experiences.

Where can I find information that keeps me up to date about technology for the visually impaired?

The Lighthouse Guild Visit the Lighthouse Guildwhich provides a great portal of information including technology available and a pod cast series about technology.
